My learning plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My goal for being at DEV is to learn how to be a full stack developer. Long term I would like to work within Aotearoa for a minimum of 1 year, potentially in the health sector or working for a well established company, where I can gain experience across the board and be on a sustainable career path. I would really like to work internationally in the tech field. My goal is to be working in England, Ireland or Scotland within the next 2 years.

Describe your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning.

A key strength as well as a limitation is my sensitivity. It means that I can be very hard on myself, and struggle to ask for help, because I have already created a list of judgments etc, “everyone” else has made about me or will make. I feel like my sensitivity (as I am sure on a global level) has really been heightened during the global pandemic and consequent lockdowns in Tāmaki. I am becoming a bit desensitized now and actively working against the roadblock of the mind everyday. I am finding coding actually quite helpful in getting out of my sensitive head and into more of an equation, pattern making world. Limitation would be sustaining long periods of focus; I am using the take of yourself and be very gentle with yourself to combat this.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Confidence in self and communication, are they key skills I would like to develop while studying. I have a terrible habit/ behaviour of putting myself down and gaslighting myself out of knowing the answer, instead of just saying it or asking the question as most likely someone else is wondering or will be. I feel like that can only grow through my time at DEV and within our homegroup and wider Horoeka cohort. I look forward to growing into myself a bit more, as time progresses.
